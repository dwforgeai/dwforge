Questions

Who built DWForge and what powers it? +
DWForge was built by enterprise integration architects with years of hands-on delivery experience across multiple industries. The platform is independently developed and owned — DWForge AI is our own technology, purpose-built for the integration space. We don't rely on or resell third-party AI tools.
How long will it stay free? +
Through beta — at minimum several months. We'll give the waitlist at least 30 days notice before introducing paid plans, and beta users always get a significant lifetime discount.
Will the generated DataWeave actually compile and run? +
Yes — every output is designed to be production-safe with proper DataWeave 2.0 syntax, null handling, and Mule event context awareness. Always review before deploying, but most outputs work directly in Anypoint Studio without changes.
Is my payload data stored anywhere? +
No. Payloads you submit are used only to generate your DataWeave in that session and are not stored, logged, or used for any other purpose.
What enterprise systems do you support? +
Currently: Salesforce (custom objects, platform events, __c/__r patterns), Microsoft D365 F&O (OData payloads, legal entity mapping), SAP, and generic JSON/XML/CSV. More being added — tell us what you need.
I have feedback — how do I share it? +
We genuinely want your feedback — good and bad. Join the waitlist and reply to our email, or reach out directly. Early feedback shapes what we build next.
