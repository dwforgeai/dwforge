Data engineering
Beyond DataWeave. Into the pipeline.

DWForge started with DataWeave because that is what we knew best. It is moving into the broader data engineering stack. Same philosophy: production-ready code, real-world patterns, no boilerplate.

01

SQL transformations

Generate Snowflake, BigQuery, Databricks SQL, and Postgres transformations from source and target schemas. Type coercion, null safety, and window functions handled out of the box.

02

ETL and ELT pipelines

End-to-end pipeline scaffolding for moving data between operational systems and your warehouse. Incremental loads, change capture, and reprocessing logic included.

03

Schema mapping at scale

Map hundreds of fields across systems without doing it row by row. DWForge infers relationships from samples and generates the mapping in one pass.

04

Stream processing

Kafka, Kinesis, Pub/Sub. Transformation logic for streaming pipelines with the same null-safety and type handling as the batch equivalents.

05

Data quality rules

Generate validation logic from your business rules. Reject, quarantine, or auto-correct bad records before they hit downstream systems.

06

One tool, full stack

The same DWForge that writes DataWeave for your Mule flow also writes the SQL for your Snowflake load and the Python for your Airflow DAG. One mental model, one tool.
